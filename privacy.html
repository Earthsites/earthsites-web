Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026>

Earthsites (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides the Earthsites mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

We aim to collect the minimum information needed to operate Earthsites and we do not sell personal information.

Information we collect

  • Account information (if you create an account): such as email or authentication identifiers.
  • App usage data: basic diagnostics and performance data to keep the app running reliably.
  • Location data (optional): only if you enable location permissions, to support location-based features.
  • Content you provide: places you save, notes, and related metadata you choose to add.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the App and its features
  • Troubleshoot bugs and maintain security
  • Communicate with you about support requests

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run the App (for example, hosting, analytics, or crash reporting), subject to appropriate safeguards.

Your choices

  • You can disable location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can request access or deletion by submitting a request via our Support form and selecting Privacy as the topic.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use our Support form and select Privacy.
